Building A Computer

Building a computer is fairly easy. It's simply like building lego but cheaper.

Here's a picture of a processor just for the sake of it. It is Intel's Kaby Lake architecture; an i7-7700k.

Parts required for putting together a P.C.

  • A Motherboard
  • A Processor (CPU)
  • A RAM Kit
  • A Case
  • A CPU Cooler (depends on which processor you get Intel's K series do not come with a stock cooler)
  • A Power Supply (PSU)
  • A Hard Drive (HDD) or a Solid State Drive (SSD)
